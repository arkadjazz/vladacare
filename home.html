Method

Trauma-Informed. Somatic. Experiential. Present-Moment.

Present-Moment Embodied Practice

About

Meet Vlada

I am a Certified Trauma Specialist, mental health professional, experiential practitioner, and author of a 2014 book that metaphorically describes the clinical model I developed early in my career.

My work is rooted in doctoral-level training in psychology and 16 years of experience with complex trauma, somatic psychology, parent–child dynamics, adolescent development, ASD, and SMI.

My passion is helping people experience themselves differently — with more safety, clarity, compassion, and connection — so they can create meaningful change in their lives and relationships.
