I am a mental health professional, Certified Trauma Specialist, and experiential practitioner with doctoral-level training in psychology and over 16 years of experience working with trauma, somatic awareness, emotional development, and complex clinical presentations.

My work integrates trauma science, present-moment experiential practice, and the body–mind connection to help people develop emotional regulation, stress resilience, and post-traumatic growth. In 2014, I authored a book that metaphorically describes the clinical model I developed early in my career—a framework grounded in emotional awareness, narrative understanding, and embodied transformation.

That early work became the foundation for the experiential method I use today.
