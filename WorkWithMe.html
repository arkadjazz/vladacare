Work With Me

Compassionate, trauma-informed experiential support designed to help you build emotional regulation, stress resilience, somatic awareness, and meaningful post-traumatic growth.

How We Work Together

I offer experiential support programs grounded in trauma science, somatic awareness, and present-moment practice. My work blends psychoeducation (so you understand your nervous system) with guided, embodied practice (so your system actually changes).

Each session is tailored to your unique life story, personality, and capacity, supporting transformation in how you feel, relate, and lead your life.
